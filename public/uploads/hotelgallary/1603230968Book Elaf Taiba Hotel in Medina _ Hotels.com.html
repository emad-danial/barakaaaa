  • Is parking offered on site at Elaf Taiba Hotel?
    Yes. Self parking costs SAR 300 per day. Parking spots may be limited.
  • Are pets allowed at Elaf Taiba Hotel?

  • What are the check-in and check-out times at Elaf Taiba Hotel?
    You can check in from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Check-out time is noon.
